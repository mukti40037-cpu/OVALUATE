Why This Product Exists

⚖️
RICS AI Standard (effective March 9, 2026) mandates ALL RICS-affiliated firms using AI for valuations to maintain a written register, governance documentation, and named human oversight — creating audit trail and liability exposure

🏦
Ovaluate's B2B API clients (banks, lenders, developers) who rely on AI valuations for lending decisions carry downstream liability if valuations prove materially inaccurate

Risk Analysis

Risks That Create the Opportunity

Every risk in the AI valuation & fractional ownership ecosystem is a product opportunity. The higher the risk, the more valuable the insurance.

⬛ Critical Risk
AI Valuation Inaccuracy
AVM outputs can deviate significantly from actual market prices — especially in thin, illiquid, or unique property segments. JLL explicitly states AI "does not replace human judgment."
→ Product 1: AVM Professional Indemnity
→ Product 3: Lender Protection Suite
⬛ Critical Risk
RICS AI Regulatory Non-Compliance
RICS AI Standard (March 2026) mandates AI registers, human oversight, signed professional judgments. Non-compliance exposes Ovaluate and its clients to regulatory action globally.
→ Product 5: Compliance & Governance Insurance
⬛ Critical Risk
Fractional Investor Concentration
Retail investors with no property expertise (e.g. Club Apparel members converting loyalty points) are exposed to property market risk with no insurance safety net — a reputational risk for PRYPCO.
→ Product 2: Fractional Ownership Protection
→ Product 4: Loyalty Investor Shield
▨ High Risk
Lender Collateral Erosion
Banks using Ovaluate's B2B API to make mortgage decisions carry risk that AI-derived LTV ratios are inaccurate. In a downturn, this creates systemic collateral shortfall.
→ Product 3: Mortgage Valuation & Lender Protection
▨ High Risk
Data Quality & AVM Model Drift
AVM accuracy is entirely dependent on data quality, recency, and model calibration. Model drift in changing markets creates systematic under/over-valuation risks.
→ Product 1: AVM PI (model monitoring clause)
→ Product 6: Parametric trigger reliability
▨ High Risk
Cross-Border Regulatory Complexity
As Ovaluate expands globally, different jurisdictions have conflicting AI, property, and financial regulations. What's compliant in UAE may not be accepted in EU, UK, or APAC.
→ Product 5: Multi-jurisdiction compliance insurance layer
▫ Moderate Risk
Rental Income Volatility
PRYPCO Blocks investors depend on monthly rental payouts from Dubai properties. Market vacancy rates, economic shocks, or specific property issues can disrupt payouts.
→ Product 2 & 4: Rental income guarantee features
▫ Moderate Risk
Platform / Counterparty Risk
Investors in PRYPCO Blocks face platform-level risk. No structural protection currently exists for fractional investors if PRYPCO ceases operations.
→ Product 2: Platform protection clause
→ Product 4: DFSA-backed ring-fencing insurance
▫ Moderate Risk
Tokenisation & Legal Ambiguity
PRYPCO Mint is a tokenised fractional ownership platform. Legal frameworks for tokenised real estate are nascent in most jurisdictions — disputes may have unclear resolution paths.
→ Legal expense insurance product for tokenised asset disputes
